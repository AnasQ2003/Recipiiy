Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Recipiyy

What is Recipiyy?

Recipiyy is a culinary platform designed to inspire and empower home cooks with thousands of recipes, cooking tips, and a vibrant community. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned chef, we provide tools and resources to make cooking enjoyable and accessible.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Login" button and selecting "Sign Up" on the login page. Fill in your details, and you'll be ready to save recipes, join discussions, and access personalized features.

Are the recipes free to access?

Yes, all recipes on Recipiyy are free to browse and access. Some premium features, like personalized meal plans or exclusive content, may require a subscription, but basic recipe access is always free.

Can I submit my own recipes?

Absolutely! Registered users can submit their own recipes through their account dashboard. Once submitted, our team reviews the recipe to ensure it meets our quality standards before publishing.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team via the Contact page. We offer email and phone support, and our team typically responds within 24-48 hours.

Do you offer dietary-specific recipes?

Yes, we have a wide range of dietary-specific recipes, including vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, keto, and more. You can filter recipes by dietary preferences on the Categories page.
