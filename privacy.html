Privacy Policy

Learn how Recipiyy collects, uses, and protects your personal information

1. Introduction

At Recipiyy, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform. By using Recipiyy, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and other details you provide when creating an account or contacting us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, such as pages visited, recipes saved, and search queries.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and device identifiers to improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our platform's features and services.
  • Personalize your experience, such as recommending recipes based on your preferences.
  • Communicate with you, including responding to inquiries and sending newsletters (if you opt in).
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance user experience and platform performance.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist with platform operations (e.g., hosting or analytics).
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Third parties with your consent, such as when you share recipes publicly.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us.
  • Opt out of promotional emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails.
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Recipiyy is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn such data has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.
